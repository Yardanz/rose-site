FAQ

1. How long does 1 order take?

Order fulfillment takes from 3 to 10 days, depending on the complexity of the order.

2. How is the price of the work determined?

The price is fixed. It takes my time, my accumulated experience, reputation, etc.

3. Can you draw arts?

No. You provide your art when ordering.

4. Is it possible to purchase a work from the showcase?

No. These are paid and completed orders of customers and belong exclusively to them.

5. Can I pay in advance (half or a third of the cost)?

Negotiated individually.
