FAQ

1. How long can my order take?

Depending on the order queue. From 3-10 days. I warn about the stages of work.

2. What does the price depend on?

On the requirements for the work, the complexity of the image, the number of characters.

3. Are you an artist? Can you draw me an art?

No. I don't draw art from 0, I only animate. You provide your image to order.

4. Can I buy an animation from Showcase window?

No. Showcase artworks are individual orders. Only the owner can use them. To purchase, go to “Shop” on the website.

5. Can I pay in advance (half or a third of the cost)?

Negotiated individually.
